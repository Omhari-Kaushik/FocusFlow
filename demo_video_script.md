# 🎥 FocusFlow Demo Video Script
**Target Duration:** ~3.5 Minutes  
**Tone:** Conversational, Relatable, Poetic yet Simple, Positive

---

### **[0:00 - 0:40] THE HOOK: The Contrast of Time**
* **Visual:** Close-up of a digital clock ticking, transitioning to the clean, dark-themed dashboard of **FocusFlow**.
* **Audio/Voiceover:**
  > *"Six months ago was the New Year. That sounds long. 
  > 
  > Six months from now is the next New Year. That feels short. 
  > 
  > If you feel this contrast, you should stay until the end of this video. We feel this tension not because time works differently, but because of our habits. The way we spend our days dictates how we perceive time. 
  > 
  > That’s why we built **FocusFlow**—a dashboard designed to help you align your daily actions and take control of your time."*

---

### **[0:40 - 1:20] CORE TOOLS: The Timer & Lo-Fi Beats**
* **Visual:** Click play on the Music Player, showing the realistic spinning vinyl record. Switch the timer tabs (Focus, Short Break) and start the countdown.
* **Audio/Voiceover:**
  > *"First, let's look at the atmosphere. FocusFlow uses a clean, glassmorphic design that just feels calm. We have a built-in lo-fi music player with real vinyl animations to help you lock in, alongside our circular Pomodoro timer. The transitions are super smooth, and every session you run here connects directly to your stats and calendar so you can see your progress build over time. And yes, the breaks are highly encouraged—because nobody can study for six hours straight."*

---

### **[1:20 - 2:05] GOALS & HABITS: Satisfying Green Ticks**
* **Visual:** Show the habits list. Click a habit to check it off, highlighting the green checkmark. Navigate to the Goals card and show a milestone progress bar.
* **Audio/Voiceover:**
  > *"Next up are habits and goals. We track daily habits using a simple 7-day view. And when you complete a habit today, you get a bright green tick. It sounds simple, but checking off that green circle feels surprisingly good. For bigger projects, you can set goals with milestone lists. The app automatically calculates your progress, so you always know exactly how close you are to finishing."*

---

### **[2:05 - 3:00] THE AI ASSISTANT: FlowAI in Action**
* **Visual:** Open the purple AI Capsule in the header. Type: *"Hey, switch to the calendar view and add a study session for tomorrow at 3 PM."* Show the dashboard automatically changing tabs and updating the calendar.
* **Audio/Voiceover:**
  > *"Now for the coolest part: **FlowAI**, your personal workspace assistant. Located right here in the header capsule, it’s powered by Google Gemini. But instead of just chatting, FlowAI can actually control your dashboard. You can ask it to add tasks, update your habits, change the music volume, or switch tabs for you. If you're feeling lazy to type and click, you just tell the AI what you want, and watch it happen."*

---

### **[3:00 - 3:35] THE CLIMAX: Chrome Extension Blocker**
* **Visual:** Turn the Distraction Shield ON on the dashboard blacklisted domains list. Open a new tab, type `youtube.com`. Instantly, the tab is blocked by the custom FocusFlow page.
* **Audio/Voiceover:**
  > *"Lastly, we have a built-in distraction blocker. But we took it a step further: it connects to a custom Chrome Extension. If you blacklist a site like YouTube and turn on the shield, the extension blocks it globally across all your browser tabs. If you try to sneak onto a distracting site, it redirects you back to your workspace with a friendly reminder to get back to work. It’s like having a study buddy who calls you out when you procrastinate."*

---

### **[3:35 - 4:00] OUTRO: Try It Out!**
* **Visual:** Show the live dashboard link on screen, then a warm wave goodbye.
* **Audio/Voiceover:**
  > *"FocusFlow is designed to make studying feel less like a chore and more like a satisfying routine. The live demo link is in the description below, and you can load the Chrome Extension in under a minute. Give it a try, and let me know what you think. Thanks for watching, and happy studying!"*
